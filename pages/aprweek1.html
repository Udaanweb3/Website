Technical Analysis - April 1st Week



Bitcoin

The past week we saw Bitcoin again touching the $48,000 mark after retracing back to sub $42,000 levels. Bitcoin was 14% up through the week, which was short-lived. After having a short flight between the bulls & bears, bitcoin again levelled down to the sub $42k level, creating panic among small traders.

BTC is respecting the Ascending channel on which it is moving since Dec 2020, with its next target of $70k in the mid-short term.BTC is forming a similar pattern to last year on the weekly chart, which is a bullish pattern.

Ethereum

For Ethereum, the price moved up 35% in the month of March followed by a dip of 9% in the starting week of April. But honestly speaking, the Ethereum chart looks a lot cheaper right now and maybe this might be the last opportunity to get in at this price below $3500.

ETH, just like BTC, is respecting the Ascending channel but is also on the way to forming a “W” or Double Bottom Pattern, which is a bullish sign for a reversal to the upside. The top of the Double Bottom Pattern might peak around the month of June-August by creating a new ATH at around $5500.

Burnly

For Burnly, since its launch in March, it has gained lots of attention with the price soaring up 616385.89% since launch. Unlike other new tokens, Burnly has been very stable throughout the month with ATH reaching a $1M Market Cap.

Burnly is at a strong support base, which is a good time for entry. Looking at the charts, it's absolutely fabulous! On the next leg up, it will surely break the ATH along with new updates coming about the project regularly.

Altcoins

There has been a sharp increase in prices of certain small-cap and medium-cap altcoins as Bitcoin Dominance has dropped by a small percentage and also because Ethereum holding strong for the past month. Always DYOR!
